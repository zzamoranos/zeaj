Evaluating Flento

Flento versus direct & indirect competitors (found by searching Flento keywords in different search engines)

Voiceflip's SaaS platform, Flento, targets fitness businesses, including gyms, personal trainers, and fitness studios, providing them with a variety of tools to ease the process of business admin work. As the fitness app market continues to grow, there's an increasing demand for apps that cater to the needs of both individual users and fitness businesses; with the market of fitness business management platforms being less saturated.

Among the likes of independent fitness apps like Nike Training Club, Nike Run Club (NTC), Trainerize, FitOn, and more recently AI-powered platforms like Zing AI in the current market - Flento exists to help fitness businesses to run successfully through bookings and calendar management features, in-app communication with clients, client file management and document intake, and automated invoicing + payment processing features. It stands out as an all-in-one solution for fitness businesses by providing them with tools catering to a variety of trainers business needs.

Market Research

In both the fitness and client relationship management space, it's hard to retain members in the long term.

Nowadays, plenty of fitness apps are available to assist those eager to improve their fitness levels. According to a recent report by Statista, the revenue generated in the Fitness segment has reached an impressive $4,024.9 million in 2021, and it continues to multiply. The Global Fitness Gym Management Software Market, characterized by a rapid and substantial growth in recent years, is also anticipated to experience continued significant expansion from 2023 to 2031, according to the Market Research Intellect. In both fitness building apps and fitness business/client relationship management, retaining members in the long term can be challenging. Many fitness businesses struggle to maintain client engagement and ultimately lose clients due to ineffective communication, lack of personalized support, and difficulty managing business operations efficiently.

Trainers' interactions with clients are heavily influenced by factors such as empathy, motivation, and the desire to help clients achieve their fitness goals. Additionally, factors like self-efficacy and confidence in their ability to effectively manage clients can impact their behavior within the app. Research by fitness industry experts like American Council on Exercise (ACE) highlights the importance of trust in client-trainer relationships and its impact on client retention and adherence to fitness programs. Every client is unique, with different fitness levels, preferences, and challenges. Trainers understand the importance of client management in tailoring fitness programs to meet each individual's specific needs and goals.
